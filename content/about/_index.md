## 个人简介

王战，博士，正高级工程师，现任浙江浙能数字科技有限公司高级技术专家、浙江省浙能工业信息工程省级重点企业研究院副院长。长期从事机器人与人工智能、新一代信息技术领域的研究与工程应用，重点面向能源行业智能巡检、无人化运维和安全生产需求，开展智能机器人、工业视觉感知、多传感融合定位与导航、工业人工智能、数字孪生和能源物联网等技术研发。

曾主持或负责国家级、省部级及企业科研项目，相关成果已在火电、海上风电、煤场、化工和钢铁等工业场景中开展应用。具有科研团队建设、项目管理、关键技术攻关、标准制定和科技成果转化经验。

## About

Zhan Wang holds a Ph.D. and is a Professor-level Senior Engineer. He currently serves as a Senior Technical Expert at 浙江浙能数字科技有限公司 and Deputy Director of 浙江省浙能工业信息工程省级重点企业研究院.

His research and engineering work focuses on robotics, artificial intelligence, and next-generation information technologies for intelligent inspection, unmanned operation and maintenance, and industrial safety in the energy sector.

His research interests include intelligent robots, industrial visual perception, multi-sensor localization and navigation, industrial artificial intelligence, digital twins, and the energy Internet of Things.

He has led or taken responsibility for national, provincial, ministerial, and enterprise research projects, with related technologies applied in thermal power, offshore wind power, coal handling, chemical, and steel industry scenarios.
