abstract: |-
  In this paper, we present an experimental testbed of a Visible Light Communication (VLC) - based localization framework developed for the 5G Internet of Radio-Light (IoRL) European project. The established testbed is capable of providing decimeter-level positioning accuracy to support high level location-based services (LBS) as well as the data transmission service for the future IoRL system. The emulation results on the testbed confirm that the proposed system achieves high location accuracy with most positioning errors less than 0.15 m and only a few more than 0.25 m corresponding to the corner area.
