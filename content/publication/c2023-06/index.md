abstract: |-
  The spontaneous combustion points of coal piles (SCP) in closed coal yards has the characteristics of dynamic expansion. Once multi-SCP arise at the same time, the choice of fire-fighting disposal path will seriously affect the coal loss and power generation cost. In response to this problem, this paper proposes a multi-SCP cooling path planning method based on genetic algorithm. The coal composition and coal yard environmental factors are used to determine the coal diffusion coefficient, combined with the parameters of fire water cannon movement and water output, the model of coal loss over time is constructed. The optimization method based on genetic algorithm is used for path planning to find fire-fighting strategy with minimal coal loss. The experimental results show that the method proposed in this paper can effectively reduce the coal loss in the process of cooling, about 30% of coal loss and 42% of cooling time can be avoided when spontaneous combustion is serious.
