## 智能机器人与自主系统 {#intelligent-robotics}

面向能源和复杂工业环境，研究巡检机器人、复合机器人、特种作业机器人及自主运行技术，重点关注多传感定位、导航避障、路径规划、机械臂控制和多机器人协同。

**Intelligent Robotics and Autonomous Systems**

Research on inspection robots, composite robots, special-purpose robots, and autonomous operation technologies for energy and complex industrial environments, with a focus on multi-sensor localization, navigation, path planning, manipulator control, and robotic collaboration.

## 工业智能感知与计算机视觉 {#industrial-perception}

研究复杂工业场景中的视觉感知与智能检测方法，包括多光谱感知、目标检测、火焰烟雾识别、跑冒滴漏检测、红外成像和设备异常识别。

**Industrial Intelligent Perception and Computer Vision**

Research on visual perception and intelligent inspection in complex industrial environments, including multispectral sensing, object detection, fire and smoke recognition, leakage detection, infrared imaging, and equipment anomaly identification.

## 工业人工智能与数字孪生 {#industrial-ai}

研究工业缺陷检测、生成模型、工业智能运维、数字孪生以及人工智能模型在能源装备运行分析和维护决策中的应用。

**Industrial Artificial Intelligence and Digital Twins**

Research on industrial defect detection, generative models, intelligent operation and maintenance, digital twins, and the application of artificial intelligence to energy equipment analysis and maintenance decision-making.

## 能源物联网与智能信息技术 {#energy-iot}

研究能源工业设备互联、OPC UA信息模型、物联网安全接入、工业通信、可见光通信与定位等技术，为能源系统的数字化和智能化提供支撑。

**Energy Internet of Things and Intelligent Information Technologies**

Research on energy equipment connectivity, OPC UA information models, secure IoT access, industrial communications, visible light communication, and localization technologies for the digital and intelligent transformation of energy systems.
